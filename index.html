About Us

Odisha Film Development Corporation (OFDC) is a pioneering organization dedicated to promoting, supporting, and fostering the growth of the film industry in Odisha. Established in [Insert Year], OFDC serves as a cornerstone for filmmakers, artists, and the creative community, ensuring that the rich cultural heritage of Odisha is showcased on the big screen.

At OFDC, our mission is to empower filmmakers by providing the necessary resources, infrastructure, and financial support to create high-quality cinema that resonates with audiences worldwide. We aim to spotlight Odisha's unique stories, languages, and landscapes through the powerful medium of film.
